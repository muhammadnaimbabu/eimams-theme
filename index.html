Welcome to eimams

Eimams is the only specialist Muslim job board in UK and perhaps elsewhere too. Vacancies from all Islamic sectors are available including Mosques, Madrasas, Islamic schools, charities and Islamic media. With bespoke search tools & specialist fields you can easily look for jobs to your liking and also advertise according to the needs of your organisation. This is the way forward for specialist Muslim job advertisement and search.
